Artist relationships are the first place where pagination and sorting matter heavily.

Endpoint references:

- [`getArtistAlbums`](/openmusic/endpoints/getArtistAlbums)
- [`getArtistSongs`](/openmusic/endpoints/getArtistSongs)

## Relationship Endpoints

| Relationship | Endpoint | Response |
| ------------ | -------- | -------- |
| Albums | `GET /artists/{id}/rel/albums` | [`AlbumPaginatedResponse`](/openmusic/endpoints/getArtistAlbums) |
| Songs | `GET /artists/{id}/rel/songs` | [`SongPaginatedResponse`](/openmusic/endpoints/getArtistSongs) |

## Shared Parameters

Both relationship endpoints support:

- `lang`
- `view`
- `limit`
- `offset`

### Artist Albums

`GET /artists/{id}/rel/albums`

Extra controls:

- `include=artists`
- `sort=name|-name|date_released|-date_released|date_added|-date_added`
- `view[albums]`
- `view[artists]`
- `limit[artists]`

### Artist Songs

`GET /artists/{id}/rel/songs`

Extra controls:

- `include=albums,artists`
- `sort=name|-name|date_released|-date_released|date_added|-date_added`
- `view[songs]`
- `view[albums]`
- `view[artists]`
- `limit[albums]`
- `limit[artists]`

## Responses

- Albums: [`AlbumPaginatedResponse`](/openmusic/endpoints/getArtistAlbums)
- Songs: [`SongPaginatedResponse`](/openmusic/endpoints/getArtistSongs)
- Errors: [`ErrorResponse`](/openmusic/responses#errorresponse)
