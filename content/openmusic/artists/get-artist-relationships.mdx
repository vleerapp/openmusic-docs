Artist relationships are the only ones where pagination and sorting is used.

Endpoint references:

- [`getArtistAlbums`](/openmusic/endpoints/getArtistAlbums)
- [`getArtistSongs`](/openmusic/endpoints/getArtistSongs)

## Relationship Endpoints

| Relationship | Endpoint | Response |
| ------------ | -------- | -------- |
| Albums | `GET /artists/{id}/rel/albums` | [`AlbumListResponse`](/openmusic/albums/responses#albumlistresponse) |
| Songs | `GET /artists/{id}/rel/songs` | [`SongListResponse`](/openmusic/songs/responses#songlistresponse) |

## Shared Parameters

Both relationship endpoints support:

- `lang`
- `view`
- `limit`
- `offset`
- `sort`

### Artist Albums

`GET /artists/{id}/rel/albums`

Extra controls:

- `include=artists`
- `sort=name|-name|date_released|-date_released|date_added|-date_added`
- `view[albums]`
- `view[artists]`
- `limit[artists]`

### Artist Songs

`GET /artists/{id}/rel/songs`

Extra controls:

- `include=albums,artists`
- `sort=name|-name|date_released|-date_released|date_added|-date_added`
- `view[songs]`
- `view[albums]`
- `view[artists]`
- `limit[albums]`
- `limit[artists]`

## Responses

- Albums: [`AlbumListResponse`](/openmusic/albums/responses#albumlistresponse)
- Songs: [`SongListResponse`](/openmusic/songs/responses#songlistresponse)
- Errors: [`ErrorResponse`](/openmusic/responses#errorresponse)
