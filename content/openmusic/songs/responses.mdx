## Related Responses

The song relationship endpoints return resource wrappers from the related type:

- `/songs/{id}/rel/albums` -> [`AlbumListResponse`](/openmusic/albums/responses#albumlistresponse)
- `/songs/{id}/rel/artists` -> [`ArtistListResponse`](/openmusic/artists/responses#artistlistresponse)
- `/songs/{id}/rel/lyrics` -> `LyricsResponse`
